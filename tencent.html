Tencent Tower, Guangzou, China

The Tencent Tower on the banks of the Pearl River in Guangzhou is set to become an icon of human life, of social exchange and opening up to the world; it should present an image of the modernity and serenity of an enterprise that’s proud of the workplace environment it has striven to create.

To achieve these goals, we’ve made eight choices that form the basis of the project.

Friendly and welcoming, Tencent Tower has a mission to enhance Guangzhou, it aims to impact the neighborhood, aspires to embellish it; it’s committed to playing its own specific urban role: it wants to turn the axis of symmetry between it and the adjacent tower to the west into a tree-lined strip that’s a social hub opening up a perspective on the Pearl River.
